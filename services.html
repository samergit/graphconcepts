Design is a lot more complicated than meets the eye. There are many important individual intricacies that go into a successful design: user experience, user interface, information architecture, overall usability, aesthetic appeal, load times, conversion rate, and more.

Despite what some might think, beauty is not the number one goal of design. It's the user experience that matters most. Digital design that users interact with cannot thought of in the same way a fashion designer may think of apparel design. Flashy design is not always better and can hinder the user experience.

At Graph Concepts, we want to create web and user-interface designs that people love using. That is our number one design goal. We create usable and beautiful designs, designs with the perfect blend of form and function.
