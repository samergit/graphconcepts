PrimeCo Wholesale was in need of a unique identity that would allow them to stand apart from the competition. We gladly took on the challenge and produced a retro yet modern identity, giving them a truly unique look in the wholesale space.

In addition to their visual identity design, PrimeCo requested a website that would showcase some of their products and business information. Considering they are a wholesale corporation that ships nationwide in the United States, we felt it was necessary to create them a professional design that was easy to use.

We also optimized their website for SEO, and they are now ranked first for the phrase "smoke shop wholesale" on Google (in the United States). In addition to the search engine optimization, we marketed PrimeCo Wholesale online, and they've reported a huge influx of new customers finding them online.
