A to Z Locksmith chose us to overhaul their website. There were two main objectives in the overhaul: the increase of conversions and the creation of a mobile site. In order to achieve these objectives, we created a completely new, fresh design.

In order to meet the objective of increasing conversions, we incorporated a customer testimonial directly into the home page, and placed a call to action button above the fold, allowing prospective clients to quickly identify they may request a free quote.

The latter objective of creating a mobile a site for A to Z Locksmith was accomplished through the use of responsive design. In the design of the mobile site, we wanted to allow prospective clients the ability to call A to Z Locksmith very quickly from their mobile phone, especially in case of emergency situations. In order to do so, we placed two very large call buttons at the very top of the mobile site.
